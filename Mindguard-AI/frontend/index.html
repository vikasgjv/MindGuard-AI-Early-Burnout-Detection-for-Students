What We Do

The early warning

MindGuard AI is an AI-powered platform that detects early signs of mental burnout and fatigue in students. By analyzing daily behavioral patterns sleep duration, study time, screen time, mood levels, and academic pressure we generate a dynamic burnout risk score and deliver personalized recommendations to improve well-being.

Fully software-based and built for scale, MindGuard AI serves universities and institutions while maintaining strict privacy standards.

The Problem

Burnout hides in plain sight

Students suffer silently while institutions lack tools to detect, intervene, or prevent burnout before it causes lasting damage.

Silent Suffering

Students experience burnout silently. By the time it surfaces, academic damage is already done.

Reactive Systems

Current wellness solutions respond only after a crisis — prevention remains an afterthought.

No Early Detection

No continuous AI system exists to analyze behavioral patterns and trigger timely interventions.

The gap between crisis and prevention

Burnout doesn't arrive overnight. It builds slowly through patterns of poor sleep, mounting academic pressure, and declining mood — invisible to traditional support systems until it's too late.

MindGuard AI bridges this gap with continuous behavioral monitoring, predictive risk scoring, and personalized nudges — acting weeks before a burnout crisis would typically be recognized.

The Process

How MindGuard works

1

Behavioral Data Collection

Daily check-ins capture sleep, study hours, screen time, mood, and academic pressure through a seamless interface.

2

AI Risk Scoring
